KEY FUNCTIONS IMPLEMENTED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

computeCredit(amount, months, annualInterestRate)
  • Calculates credit terms with optional interest
  • Generates full amortization schedule
  • Returns monthly payment, total interest, and schedule array
  • Supports 0%, 5%, 9.99%, 14.99% APR

placeOrder(orderMeta)
  • Creates order with unique ID
  • Generates full amortization schedule at order time
  • Stores complete payment history structure
  • Returns order data with schedule

addToCart(productId, quantity, paymentPlan)
  • Adds items with optional payment plan
  • Supports credit payment with interest rate selection

Payment Management Functions:
  • displayOrderHistory()       - Renders order list
  • showPaymentSchedule()       - Opens schedule modal
  • populateSchedule()          - Fills schedule table
  • processPayment()            - Handles payment processing
  • computeOrderRemainingBalance() - Calculates balance
